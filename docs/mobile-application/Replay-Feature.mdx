The **Replay Feature** allows users to visualize a vehicle’s past route for a specific time period.

---

### **Steps to Use Replay**:

- **Select a vehicle** and specify the desired **time period**.
- **Tap the Play Button** to visualize the vehicle's route on the map.
- **Customize settings**:
  - Choose the **Map Type** (e.g., Default, Hybrid, Satellite, Terrain).
  - Adjust the **Playback Speed** for a faster or slower review of the route.
