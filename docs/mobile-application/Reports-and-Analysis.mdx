The **Reports** feature allows users to generate and share detailed reports related to vehicle performance.

---

### **Accessing the Reports Page**:

- **Select a vehicle** and specify the **time period** for which you want the report.
- **Tap Show Results** to generate and view the report.

### **Sharing Reports**:

- Once the report is generated, you can easily **export** and **share** it through email or messaging for further analysis or team collaboration.

---

This feature streamlines the process of tracking and sharing vehicle data, offering flexibility in managing fleet performance.
