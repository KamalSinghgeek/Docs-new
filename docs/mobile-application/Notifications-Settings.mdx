The **Notifications Settings** section allows users to customize how they receive alerts and manage their notification preferences.

---

### **Customizable Settings**:

- **Enable or disable notifications**: Toggle notifications on or off based on your preferences.
- **Choose preferred language**: Select the language in which you wish to receive notifications.
- **Set notification sounds**: Pick from available sound options to customize notification alerts.
- **Access privacy policy details**: View the privacy policy to understand how your data is handled.
