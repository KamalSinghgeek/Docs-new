The **Vehicle List** allows you to access a comprehensive overview of all the vehicles in your fleet. Here are the features available for each vehicle in the list:

### **Key Features:**

- **Current Speed**: View the current speed of the vehicle in real-time.
- **Call Button**: Directly contact the driver or fleet manager with a tap.
- **Odometer**: Monitor the total distance traveled by the vehicle.
- **Driven Today**: Track the distance covered by the vehicle today.
- **Current Location**: See the exact location of the vehicle on the map.
- **Replay**: Replay the vehicle’s route to review past travel.
- **Directions**: Get directions to the vehicle’s current location.
- **Share Location**: Share the real-time location of the vehicle with others.

### **Why Vehicle List Matters**:

- **Fleet Visibility**: Gain real-time insights into the status and location of every vehicle.
- **Quick Actions**: Access essential features like calling the driver or getting directions to the vehicle with ease.
- **Monitor Fleet Health**: Track vehicle usage, including daily distance and odometer readings, to ensure optimal fleet performance.
