The **Reports** feature allows you to generate various reports related to your fleet's performance, vehicle activities, and more. These reports can help you analyze your fleetâ€™s data, identify trends, and make informed decisions for optimizing operations.

### **How to Access the Reports:**

- **Navigate to the Reports Section**: Open the Fleet Stack Pro application and select the **Reports** option.
- **Choose the Type of Report**: Select from different report categories, such as fuel usage, vehicle health, driving behavior, and more.
- **Download or View**: Once a report is generated, you can either view it on-screen or download it for later analysis.

### **Types of Reports You Can Access:**

1. **Fleet Overview Report**: Provides a snapshot of all vehicles in your fleet, including their current status, health, and location.
2. **Vehicle Performance Report**: Details the performance of each vehicle, including fuel efficiency, distance traveled, and idle time.
3. **Driver Behavior Report**: Analyzes driver habits such as speeding, harsh braking, and acceleration.
4. **Maintenance and Repair Report**: Tracks vehicle maintenance schedules, repairs, and upcoming service needs.
5. **Fuel Consumption Report**: Helps in monitoring the fuel efficiency of vehicles, identifying potential areas to reduce fuel costs.

These reports are essential for managers and fleet operators to keep track of operational efficiency, vehicle health, and performance, helping to lower costs and increase productivity.
