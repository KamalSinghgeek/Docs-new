The **Replay Feature** allows you to review the path traveled by a vehicle over a selected period. This feature is useful for monitoring vehicle routes, verifying locations, and understanding the movement of your fleet.

### **How to Use the Replay Feature:**

- **Select a Vehicle**: Choose the vehicle for which you want to replay the route.
- **Replay the Path**: The system will show the route the selected vehicle took, with a visual representation of the vehicleâ€™s movement over time.

This feature helps you to analyze specific trips, ensuring efficient route planning and vehicle monitoring.
