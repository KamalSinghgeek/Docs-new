The **Fleet Stack Pro Dashboard** provides a powerful, comprehensive view of your fleet’s performance. With key insights into vehicle status, health, and daily activity, the dashboard helps fleet managers monitor and optimize fleet operations. Here's a breakdown of its sections and features:

---

## **Overview**

The **Overview** section gives you a snapshot of the fleet's current status. It displays key metrics like the number of active and inactive vehicles, the total distance traveled, and AI-powered features that enhance fleet management.

### Key Metrics:

- **Total Vehicles:** Displays the total number of vehicles in your fleet.
- **Running Vehicles:** Shows the number of vehicles currently on the move.
- **Stopped Vehicles:** Shows the number of vehicles that are stationary.
- **Total Distance:** Shows the combined distance traveled by all vehicles.
- **Time:** Displays the current time to help keep track of time-sensitive information.
- **AI SmartScale:** Indicates whether AI-powered features, like predictive analytics, are enabled to help optimize fleet management.

## **Key Sections**

The dashboard is divided into several key sections to help you easily navigate through your fleet's data. These sections offer detailed insights into fleet performance, vehicle status, and other important metrics.

---

### **1. Overall Dashboard**

This section provides a summary of your fleet's most important metrics, including the total distance traveled, the current time, and the status of AI-powered features.

- **Total Distance:** Displays the total kilometers traveled by all vehicles.
- **Time:** Shows the current time for reference.
- **AI SmartScale:** Tells you if AI-powered features are currently active, helping with data-driven decisions.

---

### **2. Vehicle Status**

Here, you can see the status of all vehicles in the fleet. It helps you quickly assess whether your vehicles are active or stationary.

- **All Vehicles:** Displays the total number of vehicles in your fleet.
- **Stopped Vehicles:** Indicates how many vehicles are currently parked or inactive.
- **Running Vehicles:** Shows how many vehicles are in motion, allowing you to monitor active fleet usage.

---

### **3. Vehicle Health**

This section monitors the health of your vehicles, ensuring that they are in optimal condition for operation. It gives insights into fuel efficiency, driver behavior, and overall vehicle condition.

- **ECO Driving:** Indicates how fuel-efficient the fleet is performing. The higher the score, the better the fuel usage.
- **Driver Behavior:** Assesses whether drivers are following best practices, such as avoiding speeding or harsh braking.
- **Vehicle Health:** Monitors the health of the vehicles, such as engine condition and tire pressure, to prevent maintenance issues.

---

### **4. Weekly Data Run by Car in Kms**

This section provides a visual representation of the distances each vehicle has traveled during the past week. The bar chart helps identify usage patterns and track performance.

- **Bar Chart:** Displays the distance traveled by each vehicle in a visual, easy-to-understand format.
- **Data Points:** Shows the specific distances each vehicle has traveled, helping to identify trends and performance variations.

### **5. Bottom Navigation Bar**

The **Bottom Navigation Bar** provides quick access to essential tools and features to manage your fleet’s data and operations.

- **Status:** Displays detailed information about each vehicle’s current status (running or stopped).
- **Map:** Shows the real-time location of all vehicles on a map for easy tracking.
- **List:** Lists all the vehicles along with their details, helping you keep track of each one.
- **Utility:** Grants access to additional tools and features that enhance fleet management.
- **Other:** Provides miscellaneous options and settings for further customization.

---

## **Tips for Effective Use**

To make the most of the **Fleet Stack Pro Dashboard**, consider the following tips:

- **Monitor Key Metrics Regularly:** Check the total distance, vehicle status, and weekly data to stay on top of fleet performance and identify potential issues.
- **Utilize AI SmartScale:** Enable AI-powered features for deeper insights into fleet performance, such as predictive maintenance or optimization suggestions.
- **Improve Driver Behavior:** Use data on driver behavior to spot areas for improvement and offer training or incentives to improve driving habits.
- **Optimize Fuel Efficiency:** Focus on **ECO Driving** metrics to reduce fuel costs and improve overall fleet efficiency.
- **Maintain Vehicle Health:** Keep an eye on vehicle health metrics to prevent breakdowns, lower repair costs, and increase the lifespan of your fleet.
