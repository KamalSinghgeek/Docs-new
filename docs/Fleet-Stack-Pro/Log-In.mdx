To access your Fleet Stack Pro account and manage your fleet, follow the simple steps to log in to the app. The login process is quick and ensures secure access to your account and the dashboard.

### **Steps to Log In**

1. **Open the Fleet Stack Pro Mobile App**:

   - Launch the Fleet Stack Pro app on your mobile device. If you haven't installed it yet, download it from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).

2. **Enter Your Login Credentials**:

   - Enter your **Username** and **Password** in the provided fields. These credentials are unique to your Fleet Stack account and are required for secure access.

3. **Tap the Login Button**:
   - After entering your credentials, tap the **Login** button to access your Fleet Stack dashboard. If your credentials are correct, you will be taken directly to your fleet's dashboard, where you can start managing and monitoring your vehicles.

### **Troubleshooting Login Issues**

- **Invalid Credentials**: Ensure that you have entered the correct username and password. Double-check for any typos or case sensitivity in your login details.
